- **Subtitle**: Context about the products in the cart
- **Statistics**: Quick overview of cart contents and total value

### Main Content Area
- **Cart Items List**: Vertical list of products with clear separation
- **Product Details**: Image, name, description, and price for each item
- **Quantity Controls**: Interactive selector with increment/decrement buttons
- **Item Total**: Calculated total for each line item
- **Remove Functionality**: Clear option to remove items from cart

### Two-Column Layout
- **Left Column (60%)**: Detailed cart items list
- **Right Column (40%)**: Order summary and recommendations

### Order Summary
- **Detailed Breakdown**: Clear presentation of all costs
- **Prominent Total**: Emphasized final amount
- **Action Buttons**: Primary and secondary CTAs for conversion
- **Value-Added Services**: Promotional elements to enhance purchase

### Recommended Products
- **Cross-Selling Opportunity**: "You might also like" section
- **Product Cards**: Consistent design with other product displays
- **Horizontal Scrolling**: Efficient use of space for multiple recommendations

## Key Design Principles Demonstrated

1. **Transparency**: Clear pricing breakdown with no hidden costs
2. **Control**: Easy quantity adjustment and item removal
3. **Trust Building**: Value-added services and guarantees
4. **Conversion Optimization**: Multiple CTAs and cross-selling opportunities
5. **Visual Hierarchy**: Important elements (total, CTAs) are prominent
6. **Responsive Considerations**: Adaptable layout for different screen sizes
7. **Luxury Aesthetic**: Gold accents and premium styling throughout
8. **User Experience**: Intuitive interactions and clear information presentation

This mockup demonstrates how the shopping cart page balances functionality with visual appeal, providing users with all necessary information while encouraging completion of their purchase.
