## Design Elements Explanation

### Breadcrumb Navigation
- **Clear Path**: Shows user location within site hierarchy
- **Visual Separators**: Easy to follow navigation trail

### Two-Column Layout
- **Left Column (40%)**: Image gallery for visual engagement
- **Right Column (60%)**: Product information for decision making

### Image Gallery
- **Main Image**: Large, high-quality product visualization
- **Thumbnails**: Multiple views for comprehensive understanding
- **Interactive Elements**: Click to zoom functionality

### Product Information Section
- **Product Title**: Clear identification
- **Star Rating**: Social proof with numerical rating
- **Price Display**: Prominent with local currency
- **Special Badge**: Visual indicator for promotions
- **Detailed Description**: Key selling points
- **Feature List**: Quick scanning of benefits
- **Stock Status**: Availability transparency
- **Quantity Selector**: Customizable purchase amount
- **Action Buttons**: Clear CTAs for conversion

### Technical Specifications
- **Data Table**: Organized presentation of technical details
- **Clean Layout**: Easy scanning of important information
- **Consistent Styling**: Matches overall design language

### Social Sharing
- **Multiple Platforms**: Broad sharing options
- **Icon-Based**: Recognizable social media icons
- **Minimal Space**: Efficient use of page real estate

## Key Design Principles Demonstrated

1. **Visual Hierarchy**: Important elements (price, CTA) are prominent
2. **Trust Building**: Ratings, stock status, and warranty information
3. **Conversion Optimization**: Multiple CTAs and clear value proposition
4. **Information Architecture**: Logical grouping of related information
5. **Responsive Considerations**: Adaptable layout for different screen sizes
6. **Luxury Aesthetic**: Gold accents and premium styling throughout
7. **User Experience**: Intuitive navigation and clear information presentation

This mockup demonstrates how the product detail page balances detailed information with visual appeal, guiding users toward purchase while building confidence in their decision.
